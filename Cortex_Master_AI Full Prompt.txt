The rise of AI coding assistants has dramatically increased individual engineering productivity.

However,

AI has NOT improved team understanding.

Every engineer now has:

- Different AI tools
- Different prompts
- Different schedules
- Different reasoning
- Different assumptions
- Different mental models

As software evolves rapidly, the team's understanding slowly diverges.

The bottleneck is no longer writing code.

The bottleneck is maintaining a shared understanding of the project.

This phenomenon is called

Mental Model Drift.

This is the core problem our project solves.
